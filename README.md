## Amazon Sales Analysis Dashboard
This project is an interactive Power BI Sales Dashboard built using Amazon sales data to analyze business performance across multiple dimensions. It provides insights into sales trends, product category performance, top-selling products, customer reviews, and weekly/monthly sales patterns, helping stakeholders monitor KPIs and make data-driven decisions.

## Dashboard KPIs
📊 YTD Sales
Displays the total Year-to-Date (YTD) sales revenue generated across all products.
Helps track overall business performance throughout the year.
📈 QTD Sales
Shows Quarter-to-Date (QTD) sales, enabling comparison of current quarter performance against annual sales.
📦 YTD Product Sales
Represents the total number of products sold during the current year.
Useful for monitoring product demand and sales volume.
⭐ YTD Reviews
Displays the total customer reviews received during the year.
Helps evaluate customer engagement and product popularity.

## Dashboard Visualizations
📅 Sales by Month
Line chart displaying monthly sales trends.
Helps identify seasonality, growth patterns, and peak sales months.
📊 Sales by Week
Weekly sales analysis using a column chart.
Enables detailed tracking of sales fluctuations throughout the year.
📦 Sales by Product Category
Tabular visualization showing:
Product Category
YTD Sales
QTD Sales
Percentage Contribution to Total Sales
Helps compare category-wise business performance and identify top revenue contributors.
🏆 Top 5 Products by YTD Sales
Horizontal bar chart displaying the highest revenue-generating products.
Helps identify best-selling products for inventory and marketing decisions.
⭐ Top 5 Products by YTD Reviews
Displays products with the highest number of customer reviews.
Indicates customer engagement and product popularity.

## Interactive Features
Product Category Filter
Quarter Filter
Dynamic KPI Cards
Cross-filtering between visuals
Interactive slicers

## Business Insights
Monitor Year-to-Date and Quarter-to-Date sales performance.
Compare sales across different product categories.
Identify seasonal sales trends.
Discover top-performing products based on revenue.
Analyze customer engagement through review metrics.
Support data-driven business and inventory decisions.
